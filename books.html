Recommended Reads

Python Crash Course

Author: Eric Matthes

ISBN: 9781593279288

Pub Date: May 2019

Category: Programming / Education

A fast-paced, hands-on introduction to programming with Python. Learn how to work with data, build web apps, and automate tasks — perfect for beginners and self-learners.

The Great Gatsby

Author: F. Scott Fitzgerald

ISBN: 9780743273565

Pub Date: April 1925

Category: Classic / Fiction

Set in the Roaring Twenties, this timeless novel follows Jay Gatsby and his unrelenting pursuit of love, wealth, and the American Dream.

The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes

Author: Sir Arthur Conan Doyle

ISBN: 9780141034379

Pub Date: October 1892

Category: Mystery / Detective

The world’s greatest detective takes on twelve thrilling cases filled with deduction, wit, and suspense. A must-read for lovers of classic crime fiction.

The Mind Garden: How Habits Shape Who We Become

Author: Dr. Lillian Moore

ISBN: 9781987654321

Pub Date: February 2024

Category: Psychology / Self-Growth

Blending neuroscience and everyday reflection, this book explores how small daily habits grow into the patterns that define our identity. Practical, hopeful, and deeply human, it invites readers to cultivate awareness like tending a garden.
